Location

The HLP Lab is located in 123 Meliora Hall. The best way to get to it is to enter Meliora on the Intercampus Drive side of the building and take the East Stairwell (which is straight through the doors on that side) all the way down, then turn right and look for the signs pointing you toward 123. If you enter on the Library side of the building, then it is best to go around to the other side, else you'll have to go around to get to the East Stairwell once you hit the second floor on the West Stairwell.
